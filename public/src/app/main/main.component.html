ABOUT vektor

School of Urban Maneuver

The ever-evolving landscape of global discord is rife with complex challenges. None are more complicated than urban warfare. Despite the aversion for large-scale non-clandestine military maneuvers in densely populated areas where the potential for a tremendously high cost in civilian and collateral damage is ever present, it is undoubtedly the new paradigm of battle. The Vektor School of Urban Maneuver was developed to mitigate the risks and consequences of such endeavors. Vektor is the leading authority in educating, navigating, and circumvention of interurban conflict. Our policies and procedures are grounded in extensive cutting-edge research and in-depth case studies. We are as dynamic and as innovative as the challenges we address. We are Vektor School of Urban Maneuver.
